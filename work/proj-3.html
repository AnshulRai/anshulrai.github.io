The National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli, commonly referred to as NIT Trichy, is a premier public engineering institution near the city of Tiruchirappalli in Tamil Nadu, India. It is recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India under the National Institutes of Technology Act, 2007.

-Guide: Dr. P.Palanisamy, Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, NITT

-Involved the study of algorithms used in quantum state tomography. Assessed the algorithm's performance and worked on a possible optimization through convex methods by implementing an Alternating Direction Method of Multipliers.

-Compared algorithm performance in density matrix reconstruction for matrices of mixed state and fairly pure state, with simulation done in Octave. Potential application as a non-invasive alternative to MRI scans.

-Concepts and Tools: Linear Algebra, Calculus, Compressed Sensing Algorithms, Quantum State Tomography, Octave
